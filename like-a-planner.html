Act Like a Planner

Quickly search user-submitted ChatGPT prompts and adapt them to meet your needs.


  1. Type in your search terms. You can search for a prompt's description, language, function, type (one shot, few shot), and planning applicability

  2. Click on the grey box in the search results to select a prompt and start building.

  3. Build your own prompt by clicking on the text that reads "CLICK HERE" and filling in the requested information into the field

  4. Copy your new prompt and paste it into ChatGPT

Tip: Click the "Getting Started" text to toggle the instructions menu on/off
